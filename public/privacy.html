Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Stenth ("we", "us") is a growth consultancy operating from Canada and Australia. This policy explains what personal information we collect through stenth.com, how we use it, and the choices you have. We handle personal information in accordance with Canada's PIPEDA and the Australian Privacy Act 1988.

Information we collect

  • Information you give us — when you submit our contact form, email us, or book a call, we receive details such as your name, email address, company website, and anything you include in your message.
  • Information collected automatically — we use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics 4) that collect standard usage data: pages visited, approximate location (city level), device and browser type, and how you arrived at our site. This data is aggregated and does not directly identify you.
  • Cookies — our site and analytics tools may set cookies to distinguish repeat visits and measure marketing performance. You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings; the site will continue to work.

How we use it

  • To respond to your enquiries and provide the services you request.
  • To understand how our website is used so we can improve it.
  • To measure the effectiveness of our own marketing.
  • To send you information you have asked for, such as downloadable resources.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Ever.

Who we share it with

We share data only with service providers that help us run our business — for example analytics providers (Google), email and productivity tools, and scheduling software. These providers process data on our behalf under their own privacy safeguards. We may also disclose information if required by law.

How long we keep it

We keep enquiry and client records for as long as needed to serve you and to meet legal and accounting obligations, then delete or anonymise them. Analytics data is retained according to the retention settings of the analytics platform (typically 2–14 months).

Your rights

You can ask us at any time to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, or to stop contacting you. Email info@stenth.com and we will respond within 30 days. If you are unsatisfied with our response, you may contact the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada or the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner.

Security

We use reputable providers, access controls, and encryption in transit (HTTPS) to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we take reasonable steps to safeguard the data we hold.

International transfers

Because we operate across Canada and Australia and use global service providers, your information may be processed in countries other than your own. Where that happens, we take reasonable steps to ensure it receives comparable protection.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will post the updated version here with a new "last updated" date.
